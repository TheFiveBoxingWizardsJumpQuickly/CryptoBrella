Jarvis Drawing

Found in day 13 when click the day number. It seems to show Roland Jarvis and his vision at the age of 6.


This image seems to be part of something larger as hinted by the thumbnail for the video Roland Jarvis.

There are some dots to the left of the word Jarvis.

Solution

The dots are actually Braille.

Translated it turns into gmvnmvgstromv.

Reversing the previous message you get vmortsgvmnvmg

Using a letter replacement with this key :

G=T M=N N=M O=L R=I S=H T=G V=E

We get the word enlightenment already mentioned in Tycho's poster together with a similar image. This is actually a passcode.

Hidden Info

Looking at the EXIF of this image you find this DGCCXZIHNIHPHH.
This code was also found on P.A. Chapeau's Thought For Today 11/26/2012 http://www.nianticproject.com/?id=00cal26 Convert the letters into their position in the alphabet.

4-7-3-3-24-26-9-8-14-9-8-16-8-8.

Reverse the numbers and split them into pairs, as hinted to by the Thought for Today 11/26.

88 16 89 14 89 26 24 33 74

Add all pairs of adjacent numbers together and convert to ASCII to produce the passcode higgs29k.

Envelope

Found in a link in the Thought of the Day: Day 10 http://www.nianticproject.com/?id=00tod10 by clicking the A in PAC at the bottom

It contains the following message

k bpou kn myj zvou vnz vxr7koj myj ojzp7oxjz k njjz, vm bk22. 4p7o lp9 kz mp fjm k1qjzk1jnmz p7m pg 14 bv4, npm 9jxp1j pnj. vz 4p7 knzkxvmjz myvm myjoj bvz np gk3jz 97zfjm pn myj vzv xpnzmo7xmkpn, k zp npm 9j2kj5j myj xpnmovxmz k yv5j q7m kn q2vxj zyp72z qpzj v qop92j1 gpo 4p7 po myj nkv.

Solution

Using the following replacement

B=W F=G G=F J=E K=I L=J M=T O=R P=O Q=P R=Q U=K V=A X=C Y=H Z=D or S (depends on the spots) 1=M 2=L 3=X 4=Y 5=V 7=U 9=B

Decoded the message says: "I WORK IN THE DARK AND ACQUIRE THE RESOURCES I NEED, AT WILL. YOUR JOB IS TO GET IMPEDIMENTS OUT OF MY WAY, NOT BECOME ONE. AS YOU INDICATED THAT THERE WAS NO FIXED BUDGET ON THE ADA CONSTRUCTION, I DO NOT BELIEVE THE CONTRACTS I HAVE PUT IN PLACE SHOULD POSE A PROBLEM FOR YOU OR THE NIA."

ADA (Circle)

Original Link

Metadata

The comment field on the original JPEG contains: > SftKj lj ooj ptwh and the author field contains: > Battista Bellaso

Bellaso was the inventor of a simple cipher known as the Vigenère cipher. When the string is reversed and run through the Vigenère cipher with the key "ADA", we get a URL: http://goo.gl/jHtfP

We can do a look up and see it was only created on the 1st of November (http://goo.gl/#analytics/goo.gl/jHtfP/all_time)

This URL links to:

http://commondatastorage.googleapis.com/pac/IB/00/sc0/hung/inspired.html

Within the same page can also be found:

http://commondatastorage.googleapis.com/pac/IB/00/item/contact/Contact.mp3

and:

"In modern psychology, inspiration is not frequently studied, but it is generally seen as an entirely internal process. In each view, however, whether empiricist or mystical, inspiration is, by its nature, beyond control."

The audio files are mirrored and documented on the audio page.
