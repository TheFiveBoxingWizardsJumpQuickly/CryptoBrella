Banners (blurb)

Every day we see them on top of the board. They give us the title of the day. But on occasions there is something hidden in the banner itself.

02/24/2013

Original Link

Hidden Info

On the EXIF you can see y37zxroeopct54d
Reverse - d45tcpoeorxz73y
Skip(2) - d5coox7y4tperz3
Group by 5 and rearrange groups <1, 3, 2>
d5coo
perz3
x7y4t
Move last two chars to the front to make the passcode 4td5cooperz3x7y
