Resharing event (Ingress report ep 4)

On the 18 of April there was a chain event originating from the post about the 4th episode of Ingress Report.
The idea behind it was the following

Like a link-chain, we'd like to explore the idea of a reshare-chain. We'll select one of the public reshares of this post, then post a Power Cube passcode as a comment on that reshare at 2:30PM PDT. If there are enough reshares of that post, we'll share another. And so on.﻿

On the first post we saw the passcode 5VG3AURAU285Z﻿ posted by Brandon Badger

After that you would have to followthe ripples to get the others.

Resharing Event (Patches + Chrome Stores)

On the 19 of April there was a chain event originating from the post made by Ingress.
The idea behind it was the following

Share photos of your #Ingress patches in front of your local Chrome Hub. Power Cube passcodes will be posted on Agents' featured public photos, or reshare-chains of their posts. Purchase your patches at Chrome Hubs in SF, PDX, Chicago, and NY. Custom sewing on Chrome gear is free and done in-house.
