The Past Never Dies

In October 2014 Red released a series of movies. They had all in common the fact they were a nursery rhyme sung by a female voice distorted in a creepy way. Each video would lead to a document that seems to reveal a bit about the other Wonderland characters

01 - It's Bitsy Spider

Description
i g r c m P O R r
m u . o / Q p i

This read from top to bottom leads to the link imgur.com/PQOpRir


Hidden Info

The tile in imgur is VQVMPYXAMDMVNWTTWEINQDMNAQFU
Gronsfeld with 8 to get NINEHQPSEVENFOLLOWAFIVEFSIXM
9hqp7followa5f6m

02 - It's Raining it's Pouring

Description
1w18577q-nibotpyrc/moc.lruynit
Reverse for tinyurl.com/cryptobin-q77581w1. This will forward you to http://s29.postimg.org/nj031c2af/image.jpg


Hidden Info

The forwarder is actually a clue to another link https://cryptobin.org/q77581w1
Using the title of the video as a password reveals
//And Shahrazad perceived the dawn of day and ceased saying her permitted say.

7z4ik92//

This was a hint that the rest would come the next day (like the Shahrazad stories in Arabian Nights)
