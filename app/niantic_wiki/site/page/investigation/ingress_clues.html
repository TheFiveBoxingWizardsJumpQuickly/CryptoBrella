L3 Media
VERY_COMMON

There are a number of spelling errors in this document--whether these are intentional or not is unknown. They are as follows:

  • Page 1, paragraph 3 - "ableit" instead of "albeit"
  • Page 1, paragraph 3 - "beleive" instead of "believe"
  • Page 1, paragraph 4 - "personna" instead of "persona"
  • Page 2, paragraph 1 - "resemblence" instead of "resemblance"

The Circle symbol can be seen on the second page, overlaid upon the center of the Niantic logo.

This document mentions two projects other than Niantic--Project Whydah (also mentioned in the 11/5/2012 Word of the Day as the last known project of the NIA) and the Marie Celeste project. The Niantic, the Whydah Gally, and the Mary Celeste (referred to fictionally as the Marie Celeste) were all oceangoing ships.

Page 2, paragraph 2 is about Yuri Alaric Nagassa, a physicist that "the NIA has been monitoring on the guest lecture circuit". This could be the Professor referenced in the Abduction news clipping and part 3 of the Niantic Investigation video.
