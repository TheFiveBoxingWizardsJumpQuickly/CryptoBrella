Marfa Texas

According to Wikipedia, Marfa has relevance to the supernatural, modern art, as well as US Military operations. During WWII, thousands of pilots were trained in Marfa, and it was also the training site for US Army's Chemical mortar battalions.

Today, Marfa is a tourist attraction, known for modern art of the Chinati Foundation, as well as for the phenomenon known as the Marfa Lights.
