August and September 2013 Linked Poem Posts

Starting in August 2013, the Rekees Hturt g+ account began posting a string of potentially related posts that contained a sentence or two and an image. The posts referenced puzzles/passcodes that had not been solved as well as potential tie-ins to the storyline. Jose Ramierz collected the sentences together, forming the following:

(as of Sept. 12, 2013)

Truthseeker is what I am known
however I am not the only one
We all are truthseekers
Chasing the truth no matter what
Finding what others try to hide
i ask you all one thing
The truth might not be to your taste
You might not like what it reveals
Carry on anyway﻿
Half-truths are not enough
Only stop when you find it all
Veritas liberabit vos (The truth will set you free)
To finish my message
would like to tell you all
Unknown to you my name is
Truthseeker is what I do
who I am I cannot say. But I can
Reveal to you I am not +Klue S

EOM
