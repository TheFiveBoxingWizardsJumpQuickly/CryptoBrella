805 3NIA OPS

Posted Nov 1, 2012

Original Link
g+ link

Synopsis

On the bottom right hand corner of nianticproject.com, a phone number, 805 3NIA OPS, is listed as an anonymous tip line.

Calling that number leads to these DTMF tones.

When converted using a DTMF converter, the tones produce 46*251#6*0452, the coordinates for CERN.

Before the coordinates, the number was an NIA person of interest hotline, asking for sightings of Tycho.
