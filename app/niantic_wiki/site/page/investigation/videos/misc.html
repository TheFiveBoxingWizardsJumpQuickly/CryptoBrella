Extra Info

In the video apart from seeing some of the Escape Storyboards some footage of Jarvis Appearance at cupid span and the audio from Cosmic truth, there are some images never seen before

0m12s
It seems to be a combination of images from the large hadron collider. The year 1970 might be a reference to the year the finishing touches were put on the Standard Model of particle physics. The Standard Model describes, in a single mathematical framework, the basic constituents of nature and three of the four known forces that govern their interactions: electromagnetism; the “strong” force, which holds the nucleus of the atom together; and the “weak” force, which causes radioactive decay. In the decades since it was formulated, it has predicted the result of every experiment in particle physics, and with terrific accuracy.

0m22s
Seems to show someone looking into a collider as it glows and a powercube. It could be a reference to the epiphany night.





0m23s
Another reference to the epiphany night. the most interesting detail seems to be the man writing in the floor formulas and equations for heat capacity (thermodynamics), Schrodinger's equation (quantum mechanics), Planck-Einstein equation (describing energy of a photon), the potential height of a projectile based on it's velocity (classical mechanics), Newton's law of gravitation, variables used when describing circuits (electricity and magnetism), among others with the extra sentence "What am I missing".


0m23s
It could be Jarvis seen in a portal (or someone else)






0m25s
A image of Cern seen via google maps
