Obsessed

Obsessed is a show that follows 2 sisters that started playing Ingress as a way to feel more together, due to one living in Los Angeles and the other in New York

  • Rebecca lives in NYC. Her agent name is RBFly and her faction is the Resistance. She also has the twitter account @rblumes. You can contact her at AgentRBFly@gmail.com
  • Christina lives in LA. Her agent name is Wrenegade and her faction is the Enlightened. She also has the twitter account @ChristinaSWren. You can contact her at AgentWrenegade@gmail.com
