Other sources

In occasions there are passcodes or official puzzles that originate from other sources that do not fit any other section of the investigation of the wiki, either due to their format or sheer complexity. Those end up here. We will try to divide in the best way possible to make it easier to navigate

  • Field Trip - App from Niantic labs, for geolocation with POI
  • XM Sat Survey - interactive web-based app that seems to show XM spikes around the globe
  • Google plus - For all passcodes originated from Google+
  • Verum Inveniri - The mysterious group that has contacted PAC
  • Dead drops - Real world locations players have to go and collect something
  • Misc - Any other that does not fit the ones above
