Ultra Strike

Rarity

The rarity of Ultra Srike is very common, meaning they can be easily obtained via hacking. The level you obtain is limited by your own level and the level of the portal.

Recycling

Recycling an Ultra strike will give you 20 XM x the Ultra Strike lvl
