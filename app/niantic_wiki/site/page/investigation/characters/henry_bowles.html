Henry_Bowles

When sending an e-mail to hyperbowles@gmail.com one would receive this auto-reply:

Hi there.

Thanks for reaching out to me, I'm currently unavailable as I'm working on a large scale government funded development project and may not have regular access to my personal email account during this time frame.

I look forward to responding to you as soon as I can.

H.B. Developer 141

The last known auto-response was recieved on the 7th of July, while as of the 4th of November it appears to be disabled.
