13 Magnus

Items listed in general chronological order of when they were leaked to agents. See the 13 Magnus Timeline for a timeline of 13 Magnus events in history.

Overview

Potential Project/Organization. Also Operation #13Magnus is name given to October - December 2013 anomaly event

Dr. Calvin Ezekiel, working with an unknown organization in the 1990s, was tasked with finding the Threats and Opportunities in Secret Societies. He sends an internal memo asking for funding to followup on information from a Humint source (now deceased through potential suspicious circumstances) about a secret society he gives a code name in the memo of 13Magnus. 13Magnus infiltrates other secret societies. This society has splintered and reformed through history.
