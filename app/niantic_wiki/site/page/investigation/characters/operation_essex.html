Operation Essex

Operation Essex is a group created by Dr. Edgar Allan Wright, to get players to investigate the mysteries of the Niantic Project, with some support from official sources.


Anyone can join by finding the current set of questions (listed in the g+ group, which can be seen publicly) and messaging any member of Essex with the answers.

An overview of the resources shared by members can be found Operation Essex Resources

It was first mentioned in this g+ post

The fever of yesterday has passed, leaving clarity.

I must throw everything I have into this investigation. I don't have money like the corporations. I don't have data like ADA or Omnivore. I don't even have the intel of the NIA, nor to I benefit from the knowledge of those who were actually on the Niantic Project. But I do have, at my disposal, the best investigators in the world. I commence Operation Essex in memory of an ancestor.

It is our mission to assemble all of the data we have and follow the Ingression... For the future the present and the past depend on our success.

Who will join me in Operation Essex?

Origin of the name

Edgar explained in this post

Somebody asked about the significance of the Essex: One of my ancestors was aboard that ship. The Essex went down when rammed by a whale and was the stimulus for Moby Dick. I trying to stop the Leviathan that is coming, I feel like Captain Ahab. My ancestor, by the way, survived the Wreck of the Essex and died in a hurricane alter on.

This is a reference to William Wright that after surviving the Essex incident returned to whaling and drowned during a hurricane in the West Indies
