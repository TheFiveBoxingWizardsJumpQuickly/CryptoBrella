Transcript

T-32 days to #Recursion .

This is my last entry before the Anomaly at Santa Fe.

ADA contacted me. She offered to preserve my memories, all the knowledge I have obtained in the last three years and to provide that back to me after the #Recursion .

I am still pondering this. I want to remain who I am, but I am worried about why she is offering to help.

She seems to believe my knowledge will help her in other ways. God help us if that is true.﻿
