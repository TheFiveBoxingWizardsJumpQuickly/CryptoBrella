Resistance Shard Operations

Resisting the Resurrection of Jarvis

The 13Magnus Anomaly unleashed 13 Jarvis Shards around the world. For 6 weeks, the Resistance fought to move the Jarvis Shards to their goal portal in Buenos Aires, Argentina. Oliver Lynton-Wolfe claimed that he could destroy Jarvis permanently once a viable amount of Shards were gathered at the Piramid de Mayo. The following operations document the coordination and hard work of Resistance agents to deliver the Jarvis Shards to Piramide de Mayo and to block the Enlightened from their goal at Cupid Span.
