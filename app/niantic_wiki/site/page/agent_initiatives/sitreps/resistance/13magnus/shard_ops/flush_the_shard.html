Operation: Flush The Shard

Objective: Prevent Shard #1, “Awareness” from moving closer to San Francisco and instead redirect it closer to Agentina.

PreEvent

FirstDegree is actively participating in Shard #1 monitoring with other leaders from the SouthWest. Shard has been moved to Austin, TX; however, it looks like Resistance have it under control and a plan is in place to move it the appropriate direction, closer to Argentina.

Event Timeline

All timing below is approximate pst.

Monday, November 18, 2013

19:00 Agent FirstDegree is enroute from Las Vegas to the St. George, UT for personal reasons when he receives notification that the Enlightened have managed to get control of Shard #1 and have moved it to the St. George Area.

19:15: FirstDegree notifies St. George and Las Vegas Resistance Agents that the Shard is in our area and makes arrangements to meet St. George agents near the Shard as soon as he can arrive.

19:30: FirstDegree stops in Mesquite, NV and puts a blocking link in place between Mesquite, NV and Wendover, NV. Effectively blocking nearly the entire NV/UT border, preventing further movement of the Shard to the West. He continues on his way to Utah.

20:00: The Enlightened move the Shard from Hole in the Wall to Southern Exploring Company in Hurricane, UT.

20:30: Agents Submarginals and ArrMiHardies perform Intel Op functions to determine that with a little local clearing, there’s a clear link path available to link from Hurricane, UT to a portal in Nipton, CA. Putting it firmly in Las Vegas Resistance hands. Submarginals is dispatched to travel to Nipton to receive the Shard with the assumption Agents on site in Utah will be able to move it.

20:50: Agents EVILoptimist, DChamp, sunvalleyopi, and FirstDegree arrive at the portal. FirstDegree attempts to use an ADA Refractor to take the flip option off the table at the top of the hour; however, the flip card fails for unknown reason. The four Resistance Agents instead initiate an attack on the portal with two Enlightened Agents defending. The Resistance is able to take control of the portal at 20:55.

20:58: A Jarvis virus is successfully used by the Enlightened returning the portal to their hands. They establish a link to another portal. The Resistance resumes their attack drop the link at 20:59 and regain control at 21:00 exactly. The battle continues with the Enlightened trying to regain control with the Resistance defending through 21:01 at which point both sides halt further action.

21:10: With the Resistance firmly in control of the portal, one Enlightened agent leaves the portal to place blocking links in Hurricane to prevent Resistance movement.

21:30: EVILOptimist is dispatched to clear the blocking links to allow a Shard movement at 22:00.

21:50: ArrMiHardies is prepared in Las Vegas to drop blue blocking links to allow a Shard movement at 22:00.

22:00: Green blocking links remain in place preventing Resistance movement. Agents from both factions listed above return and both factions actively fight over the Shard portal from 21:55 through 22:00. Resistance continues to hold control of the portal. EVILoptimist again is dispatched to clear remaining blocking links.

22:57: Blocking links having been successfully cleared, FirstDegree successfully links from the Shard portal to the Nipton, CA portal. The two Enlightened Agents attempt to use another Jarvis, which fails. They have to resort to a standard attack. Resistance is able to hold the link in place until 2 seconds before the Shard jump, when the Enlightened successfully knock off the link; however, are unable to take possession of the portal. Battle subsides at 23:01. Submarginals continues to wait in Nipton. sunvalleyopi has to leave and is replaced by 00nerspism.

23:30: With both groups of agents sitting in adjacent vehicles, the Resistance Agents identify they are missing something. 00nerspism walks over to the Enlightened vehicle and knocks on the window to ask, “Do you have any Grey Poupon?”.

23:58 The Enlightened begin their attack. The Resistance defend while FirstDegree having expended his one Nipton key, attempts to link to Mineral Park instead with the expectation Submarginals will then link from Nipton to Mineral Park to further the Shard. The link while showing “Link Established” with the animation in the client fades and fails to move the Shard even though ownership of at least three resonators on the portal was retained through the attack and the key is missing from Inventory. The battle again subsides around 00:02.

Tuesday, November 19, 2013

00:20: EVILoptimist identifies that FirstDegree had given him a Nipton key in the past. They will attempt to link to Nipton at 01:00. One of the Enlightned agents has to leave leaving the portal defended by a single defender.

00:55: FirstDegree again attempts to place the link to Nipton. Link fails with the same symptoms as before, he then attempts to link to Mineral Park and Chloride, both fail.

00:58: The Enlightened begin their attack residing at 01:02. Resistance again retain possession of the portal, but are unable to form links.

01:37: Agents Vegasmaize, xedis, and ToadSmasher plan to leave Las Vegas to travel to Hurricane for the next shift or to help with defense to allow the link to be made.

01:55: EVILoptimist attempts to form the link, but has the same problem.

01:58: Enlightened begin their attack. The Resistance attempt a new defense strategy and are able to hold the portal without losing a single resonator (they suspect the Enlightened are short are high level resources) through the entire attack with it subsiding at 02:02.

02:10: Resistance have Agents DChamp and 00nerspism also attempt to link to various nearby local portals without blocks, but get the same result. Client shows “Link Established”; however, it then immediately fades without the link actually showing up. Resistance Agents approach the Enlightened Agent on site. They notify him, they’ll let him take the portal for the purpose of attempting to link to see if it will work. He takes the portal and attempts to link to a local green portal and also fails. FirstDegree makes a video showing the problem and posts it on G+. All agents leave the scene having determined something is “broken” with the Shard portal. Submarginals leaves Nipton.

03:00: FirstDegree travels to another portal in the area and attempts to link back to the Shard portal. Receives the same problem.

01:00: The Shard successfully jumps to Lake Havasu City, AZ and into the capable hands of the Valley Resistance Militia.

Outcome

Las Vegas Resistance along with St. George Resistance successfully intercepted Shard #1 taking it from the Enlightened hands and routed it significantly further away from San Francisco. In the process, the Las Vegas Resistance demonstrated our local dominance and showed the world and the Enlightened that we will not allow any Shards to traverse our territory to get closer to San Francisco. While it was a very frustrating experience due to problems with linking, the Las Vegas Resistance will not give up. All involved in the battle are HEROES in our on-going struggle against alien influence. We will always RESIST!!!

All told, we had the Shard in our area under active control for 52 hours. With this newly obtained experience, we are ready to again dominate the Shard should another one come into our range. Jarvis will not be resurrected on our watch.
