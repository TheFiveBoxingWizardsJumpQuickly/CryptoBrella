Operation 3JM G+

The journey of Jarvis from Johor to Sabah, Malaysia.

The Jata Johor Portal is Johor's coat of arms consisting of a central shield topped by a coronet, sided by two rampant tigers. As Shard #4 was in the neighborhood, Team Johor Tigers coordinated an operation to lure the shard from Senai, Johor to this meaningful portal (tigers also being the symbol for the Johor Enlightened), before sending it to its next destination.

The entire operation took approximately 11 hours from start to finish, with the Shard being lured from Senai at 5 pm, being corralled by Team Enlightened Singapore back after a momentary diversion, and arriving at Jata Johor at 12 am.

The Johor Tigers powered up this portal to level 8 in a magnificent emerald glow (complete with live props) to welcome Jarvis Shard #4 to the heart of Johor Enlightened. After spending a few hours with Team Johor Tigers and embracing the strength of the Malaysian Enlightened for Roland Jarvis’s reunification, Team Johor Tigers then dispatched Shard#4 on to its next destination of Kota Kinabalu, Sabah at 3.00 am.
