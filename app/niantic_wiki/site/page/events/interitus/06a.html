#Interitus 06 - Krakow, Bergen, Saint Petersburg Summary of Anomaly Conditions @ M1:

Krakow - R:233 - E:195 Bergen - R:28 - E:44 St. Petersburg - R:41 - E:60

Total - R:302 - E:299

The final #Interitus Anomaly: Resistance and Enlightened Agents, across the globe, are once again rising to the challenge in the struggle to either ensure ADA’s evolution and growth, or forestall it to prevent what Roland Jarvis has called ‘The Interitus,’ the rise of machine intelligence and subsequent decay of the Portals and their influence over mankind.

At Measurement 1, this Anomaly is a tense standoff, with the Resistance leading by merely 3. The Enlightened are effectively leveraging the Satellite Sites to offset a slight Resistance upper hand in the Primary City of Krakow.
