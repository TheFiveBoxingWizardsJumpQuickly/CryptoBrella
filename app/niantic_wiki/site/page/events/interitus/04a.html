Repercussions

It all came down to Brisbane.

The struggle in Melbourne continued till the end, with the Enlightened maintaining an upper edge throughout the day. Across the world, Enlightened Agents rose to the challenge, capturing a majority of the Cells at stake in this Anomaly. In the final moments, however, literally minutes before the Measurement 4 window opened, the Resistance successfully executed a takedown of the Auckland field by attacking its eastern anchor. They went on to build up a dense network of 75 links in the moments before the Measurement struck.

In the end, Brisbane and the overwhelming Enlightened presence there enabled the Faction to capture Anomaly 01.

Hats off to all involved. This one was hard fought, but it’s not over yet...
