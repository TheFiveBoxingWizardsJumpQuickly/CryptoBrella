Set-Up

Repercussions

The day began well for the Enlightened. Following a massive operation covering all three Anomaly 1 Sites in Enlightened MegaFields, they went on to leverage them effectively in Melbourne and Brisbane. In Auckland, the Resistance pushed back hard, taking down the one of the Fields in the final moments and leveraging Links to dominate the site. Globally, Enlightened Agents struggled for and captured a majority of the Cells associated with Anomaly 1. As we entered Anomaly 2, the Enlightened led by more than 200, what seemed to be a comfortable and defensible position.

Then, the winds changed. The Enlightened attempted a repeat performance, casting massive Fields over Cheyenne, but without effective ground support their impact was mostly negligible. In Kansas City and Cheyenne, Resistance forces swarmed the Clusters, effectively shutting the Enlightened out at every corner. In Sioux Falls, Enlightened Agents struggled to keep pace, but were outperformed by the Resistance there, as well. Finally, in a stunning show of force across the Globe, the Resistance captured every Cell associated with Anomaly 2 spare one.

In the end, the Enlightened advantage from earlier in the day was decimated. The Resistance captured the day with a massive lead of nearly 1500.
