Circle

1 - current analysis has 17 sets of 60 ternary bits, based on the concentric circles, clockwise

2 - current analysis has 17 sets but there is a weird half-size circle in there that gives us 18 sets

3 - it may be 60 sets of 18 ternary bits, whose order (in-wise or out-wise) is not clear.
