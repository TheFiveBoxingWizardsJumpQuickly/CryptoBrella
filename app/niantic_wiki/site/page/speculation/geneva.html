tmargo's Theory

XM Sat Survey

The Chrome WebGL app shows many geo-locations on a globe with corresponding magnitudes for each point. There are 3 surveys. The 1990 survey has points distributed throughout the whole globe. The 1995 & 2000 surveys have points only clustered around Geneva, Switzerland. Two CERN buildings are targeted (Bldg 39 & 41). These both have CERN hotels in them. The first point in the stream (which also has the highest magnitude) is a bit removed from the rest of the cluster.

Could this be where Loeb is headed? Where Tycho is hiding? If anyone is near Geneva, please use Ingress and Field Trip to check these locations and report findings here.
