#Nominus

As an apparent result of the end of Acess Dive 3 VI initiated the #Nominus deaddrops.

These are 15 pieces of a script written by Andy Nominus.

Each piece was located by images shared by VI of locations around the world.

Also each piece has in the back a number in a sequence X/15 (example 01/15) that matches the number in the post and also a sequence of letters and numbers that seem that they could be used for a metapuzzle.


A bit after the first post Misty posted the following

"I've heard that more pages of that screenplay have dropped. While I find it fascinating to read an account of an event that I believed was unknown. Worries me a little. These are dangerous people, and you could get a girl killed. Remember the Magic Castle.﻿"

Later she would post

"Upon further thought, I think that the importance of getting the truth about #riphankjohnson out supercedes whatever threat to my safety might be involved. I can usually see them coming and I'm a survivor. So #operationessex and +Verum Inveniri let the truth out. Great detective work, by the way.﻿""




The Niantic Project would also post

+Verum Inveniri claim to have obtained further documentation produced by Andy Nominus, the sensitive screenplay author who first revealed the Epiphany Night incident at the Niantic Project to us in detail.
Early reports claim this document may shed critical light on +Hank Johnson.






As an extra note VI changed their banner during the event

Hidden Info

Getting all the letters and numbers in order from the backs of the pieces you get artifact6so09fr
To get the missing number the hint 8609 has to be used. This is a hint that revealed that all VI format passcodes last digit is a checsum digit. The formula for the last digit is length of keyword + first 3 numbers. Round up that result and then subtract from that value the result you had.
In this case 8+6+0+9 = 23.
Round up = 30
30-23 = 7
artifact6so09fr7
