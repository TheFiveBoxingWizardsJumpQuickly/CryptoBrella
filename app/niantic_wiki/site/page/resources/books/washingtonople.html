Washingtonople

A world-famous, fictional archeologist uses classified maps to prove that three key monuments along the National Mall -- the White House, the FDR Memorial and the World War II Memorial (under construction) -- are astronomically aligned to the three belt stars of the Orion constellation. Furthermore, he argues that these terrestrial landmarks soon will "lock" with their celestial counterparts at a date predetermined by the Founding Fathers. The message is clear, Dr. Conrad concludes: America's date with destiny draws nigh. Are you ready?
