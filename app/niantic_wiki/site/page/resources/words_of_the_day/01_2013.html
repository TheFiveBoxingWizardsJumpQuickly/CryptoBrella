01/01/2013

PRECONSCIOUS MEMORIES
Unconscious memories that can be retrieved into consciousness when necessary.

01/02/2013

TACHYON
TACHYONIC PARTICLE
A hypothetical particle that always travels faster than light. The word comes from the Greek tachys, meaning rapid, quick, fast or swift.

Hidden Info

7sr2rwetatm5xf8 is hidden in the source. doing a quick anagram and reversing it you can obtain 8xf5matterw2r7s just replace the word matter for exotic and we obtain a new passcode - 8xf5exoticw2r7s

01/03/2013

DEJA VU
That eerie sense that you have lived this before.

One plausible explanation based on current research is that Deja Vu may be an XM induced glimpse into an alternate reality.

01/04/2013

ARTIFACT
Any object that has been made, modified, or used by people.

01/05/2013

SATISFACTION OF SEARCH
A term from the field of radiology.

The phenomenon wherein the discovery of an interesting or anomalous fact prevents the observer from noticing the remaining important details of a system due to a false sense of completion.

Hidden Info

N{f22845eada6b7a70205581f9edbe1b} can be found in source
reversing the f22845eada6b7a70205581f9edbe1b you get b1ebde9f18550207a7b6adae54822f.
Translating from Hex to base64 you get sevenxhVAgentq2uVIIv
This can be translated to a new passcode - 7xh5agentq2u7v

01/06/2013

CLANDESTINE SERVICE
The arm of the CIA responsible for espionage operations that utilize human assets.

01/07/2013

PETROGLYPHS

Ancient carvings in rock which are believed to express artistic or religious meaning.

Hidden Info

In small letters we got nnqgthcitnainerpxfrxsxs
Reverse the middle word and we can divide it into 3 bits. after that we can make the following logic conversion for numbers and the middle word
nnqgth => 9qg3
preniantic => whydah
xfrxsxs => x4x6s

This is our new passcode - 9qg3whydahx4x6s

01/08/2013

OTS

The Office of Technical Service
Formerly known as the Technical Services Division, OST is the CIA's technical arm of the Clandestine Service.

It is responsible for developing and deploying the tools required for covert operations.

01/28/2013

FOLIE A DEUX
An altered cognitive or emotional state transferred between people who are closely associated with one another. A form of synergistic pareidolia.

Hidden Info

This can be found at the end of the word pareidolia
e5z5fvckuex9btl5

Small 1px text: e5z5fvckuex9btl5
Replace numbers with corresponding letter positions in the alphabet: eezefvckuexibtle
Reformat into a 4x4 square:
eeze
fvck
uexi
btle
Read clockwise in a spiral path starting with 'b': bufeezekieltevcx
Replace letters with a numbered alphabet: 2uf5ezekielt5v3x (Ingress App passcode).

01/29/2013

ZEIGNARIK EFFECT
The psychological need to complete an unfinished task.

Hidden Info

This can be found at the end of the sentence
vireelainedpbtfz

Small 1px text: vireelainedpbtfz
Replace Roman numeral vi=6 and remaining letters with a numbered alphabet (e=5, b=2, f=6): 6re5lainedp2t6z
Reverse middle word: 6re5denialp2t6z (Ingress App passcode).
