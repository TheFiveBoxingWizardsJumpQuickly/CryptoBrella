MATRIOSHKA BRAIN
A megastructure of immense computational capacity using a Dyson sphere system for energy. One potential use would be to run uploads of human minds into virtual reality spaces. Often seen as an alternative to the Jupiter Brain.

Hidden Info

t.Zw.Ane.AfBp.owxoD.
Do a pyramid: 1 char, 2 chars, etc. . The bottom row, read right-left, becomes the first column, top to bottom. (the dots should now be on TOP of each column. Now, apply a shift to each row of letters, according to the row number (D shift 1, op shift 2, etc.). Capital letters become the numbers (E > 5, F > 6). Put it all together, read left to right, top to bottom 5qra5hajrat665y
