= Sphere of Weirdness (11/2012) =

11/01/2012

Conspiracy Board
aka Investigator Board
A board, sometimes physical, sometimes digital which is used by an investigator to 'connect the dots' in a conspiracy which has several different, seemingly unrelated individuals and activities.

11/02/2012

BLACKDEV
A secret device, often created by an intelligence agency whose existence is denied or disavowed.

11/03/2012

Deep Throat
A secret informant with intimate knowledge of a situation. From the Book "All The President's Men" by Woodward and Bernstein.

11/04/2012

Ingress
A means or place of entering.
