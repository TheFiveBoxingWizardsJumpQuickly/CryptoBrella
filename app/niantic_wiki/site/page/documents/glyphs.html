Transcript

BULLETIN SHAPER GLYPHS RP:PORTED DISCOVERED IN INGRESS SCANNER DEVICE ALERT : IF YOU DISCOVER SHAPER GLYPHS IN YOUR DEVICE PLEASE NOTIFY US BELOW.

We are attempting to confirm reports that code was discovered in the Ingress Scanner Device that, disguised as a tool to improve Portal Hacks, exposes Agents to a series of Shaper Glyphs. It is believed that this code may be the work of Dr. Oliver Lynton-Wolfe, perhaps developed during his time at Hulong Transglobal, but we are investigating other theories.

Agents should be advised that exposure to Shaper Glyphs is dangerous. Shaper Glyphs are suspected as having contributed to the tragic fate of former Niantic Project investigator, Carrie Campbell, and may also be suspect in an incident involving the investigator, Klue.

Although researchers have determined that Portal Hacking is improved through use of the Glyphs,cannot provide assistance to Agents who experience unwanted side effects from exposure to Shaper Glyphs, and advises all Agents to exercise extreme caution.

If Shaper Glyphs appear on your scanner, please notify us . We are trying to ascertain the scope of this development .
