Hidden Info

Adjusting the levels you can obtain this image.

On the top right corner you can see a series of dots dashs and equal signs. Changing all equal signs to 2 dashes (= to --) you get:
.--....-.--..--..---..-..--....-.--.--.-..--...-..--..--.---.-..
Assigning 0 to each dot and 1 to each dash:

0110000101100110011100100110000101101101001100010011001101110100

Converted to binary and then to ASCII you get the passcode afram13t.
