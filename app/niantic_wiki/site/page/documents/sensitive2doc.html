Adjusting the levels you can obtain this image.

You can then see in red the numbers
8312 9860 6570 8249 4376 1574 6374 9572 2130 4685 8594 1286 6297 0835 2651 7083 2372 6495
Converting the even numbers to 0 and the odd numbers to 1 we get
0110 1000 0110 0001 0110 1110 0110 1110 0110 0001 0110 1000 0011 0011 0011 1001 0110 0011
Converting this binary to ASCII we get hannah39c which works as a Ingress passcode
