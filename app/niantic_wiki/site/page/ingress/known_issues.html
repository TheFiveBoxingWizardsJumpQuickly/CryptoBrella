Known issues with Ingress

Note: these issues are issues I know have come up, not that Google necessarily knows about. One topic is enough for each, though!

App Only

  • Faction selection ignored - sometimes you will not get the choice to pick a faction, or you will be placed into the other faction to the one you chose (e.g. https://groups.google.com/forum/?fromgroups=#!topic/ingress-discuss/ri5l3CVRcQM)
  • Unavailable in certain countries - not a bug? Sideload the APK, if you really want. Portals will likely be heavily limited.
  • Can't pan/zoom
  • Tutorials broken in various different ways

Website (intel site) only

  • Website is slow
