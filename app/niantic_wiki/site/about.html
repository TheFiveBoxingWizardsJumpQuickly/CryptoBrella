About this archive

This is an unofficial archive of the Ingress story wiki originally hosted at https://niantic.schlarp.com/.

All original content belongs to its respective authors and contributors.

This archive is provided to preserve access after the original site became unavailable.

The content has been converted from dokuwiki to static HTML. While efforts have been made to preserve the original structure and content, some differences in layout or navigation may remain.

If you are an original author or contributor and would like any content to be modified or removed, please contact: https://x.com/a3xgvo

This archive is hosted as part of Cryptobrella (https://www.cryptobrella.app/), a site providing Ingress-related content such as passcode challenges.
